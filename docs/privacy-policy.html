Privacy Policy for YungMusic

Last updated: May 28, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is handled in YungMusic.

1. Data Collection and Usage

Local Data Storage

YungMusic is designed to be privacy-first. The following data is stored only locally on your device:

  • Your listening history
  • Your search history
  • Your downloaded/cached music files
  • Your playlists and library preferences

You can clear this local data at any time through the app's Settings menu.

Analytics and Crash Reporting

To help improve the app, we use Google Analytics for Firebase. This collects anonymous usage data, such as which features are most used, and crash reports. You can opt-out of analytics within the app settings.

Advertising

YungMusic uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use device identifiers and other data to provide personalized ads. You can manage your ad preferences in your device settings.

2. Third-Party Services

The app communicates with several third-party APIs to provide its features:

  • Lyrics Providers: (e.g., LRCLIB, KuGou) Song titles and artists are sent to fetch lyrics.
  • Last.fm: If you enable the Last.fm integration, your listening history is sent to Last.fm for scrobbling.

3. Permissions

The app requires the following permissions:

  • Internet: To stream music and fetch metadata.
  • Notifications: To show the media playback controls in your notification shade.

4. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
